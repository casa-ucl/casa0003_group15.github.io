Vaccination Process

According to Sky news, London boroughs are estimated to have the lowest COVID vaccination rates for over- 50s populations, where Stratford-on-Avon is believed to have all over 50s population vaccine.


As financial times states, Vaccination rates in London’s ethnically diverse boroughs are much lower than in the rest of the country. Also, the population in ethnic groups includes Black African, Black Caribbean, Bangladeshi, Pakistani and Chinese are much more likely to have not been vaccinated. These ethnical-diverse areas were hit hardest by the pandemic and also least likely to take the vaccine. Campaigner Patrick Vernon states that “social inequality and structural racism” with health inequalities faced by non-white residences exist during the pandemic, which he argues is the reason why these boroughs have such a low vaccination rate.
